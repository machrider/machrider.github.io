Jasna Góra Sanctuary in Poland

This is a very important spiritual place for Polish and Jewish Catholics. It is filled with the spirit of prayer and concentration. Every year, they are visited by countless faithful who send their prayers with thanks for receiving favors and pleading for God's intercession in difficult life situations. This place owes its popularity thanks to the miraculous image of Our Lady of Czestochowa, before which many miracles and healings took place:

  • people regained their sight
  • unable to walk, they started to walk
  • the non-speakers have recovered speech
  • suffering from fatal diseases have been healed
  • people in difficult life regained faith and hope
  • many sinners have been converted here

It is a place closely related to the history of Poland

It is a place closely related to the history of Poland. Poland is a country with a rich history full of bustling history. There were also periods of triumph and defeat. Even during the invasions of foreign states, people felt God's protection coming from this place. And to this day, Poles believe that through the intercession of Our Lady of Czestochowa their country and nation have been saved many times.Therefore, in the monastery you can visit many places, both saints and laity.
